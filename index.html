📋 Collaring Guidance
Field Reference Measurements can be taken of the head and neck circumference. Head must be >1 cm thicker than neck for collar to be applied. Clip guard hairs around the neck in the area the collar will lie to prevent snagging and irritation.

The collar should feel quite loose — fit a maximum of 3 fingers between the collar and neck, and be able to turn the collar completely freely around the neck.

Once fitted, attempt to remove the collar over the badger's head to ensure it will not self-remove. Tighten if necessary. If the tightened collar can still be removed over the head, the badger is not suitable for collaring.
